ABOUT THE CLUB

Key features of our club

Membership

Membership in the Rotaract Club of Sololo is composed of young like minded individuals originating from the Marsabit and Isiolo counties

Dues

In addition to the time commitment of attending fellowships, members are required to contribute KES 200 per month and actively participate in projects organized by the club. This ensures continued engagement and support for the club’s initiatives.

Passion

The Rotaract Club of Sololo "Hope of the North " Our ultimate goal is to create sustainable and lasting impact within our community which is marginalized society through community service projects, leadership, and development. As part of Rotary International, the club aligns with the broader objectives of taking action through community and international service, learning leadership skills, and gaining a global perspective. By fostering professional development and cultivating like-minded young leaders, the club aims to implement sustainable solutions that address local challenges, ultimately empowering the community and driving meaningful change.
